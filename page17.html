MULTIPLE GAME

Fun Learning With Digit

Multiple games in this site are virtual and simulation games which provides environment of playing and learning for student who learn about Sistem Digit or Digital Electronic. Either online or offline game whichever student can choose consist with integration of skill, chance and strategy to simulate the reality aspect of virtual learning. This site also provide design of teaching that implement computer board or fully computerized device.
